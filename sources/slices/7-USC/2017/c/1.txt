7 U.S.C. 2017(c)(1) First month benefits prorated

The value of the allotment issued to any eligible household for the initial
month or other initial period for which an allotment is issued shall have a
value which bears the same ratio to the value of the allotment for a full month
or other initial period for which the allotment is issued as the number of days
(from the date of application) remaining in the month or other initial period
for which the allotment is issued bears to the total number of days in the
month or other initial period for which the allotment is issued, except that no
allotment may be issued to a household for the initial month or period if the
value of the allotment which such household would otherwise be eligible to
receive under this subsection is less than $10.
