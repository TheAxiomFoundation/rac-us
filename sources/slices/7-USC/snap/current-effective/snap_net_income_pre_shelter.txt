SNAP pre-shelter net income under 7 USC 2014(e)(6)(A) for period 2024-01.

Use monthly SNAP period 2024-01.

Under 7 USC 2014(e)(6)(A), the excess shelter deduction compares shelter costs
to 50 percent of monthly household income after all other applicable deductions
have been allowed.

Model `snap_net_income_pre_shelter` as monthly household income after all other
applicable deductions have been allowed.
