7 USC 2014(g)(1) SNAP asset test

(g) Allowable financial resources
(1) Total amount
(A) In general
The Secretary shall prescribe the types and allowable amounts of financial
resources an eligible household may own, and shall assure that a household
otherwise eligible for SNAP will not be eligible if its resources exceed the
statutory asset limits, subject to the annual inflation adjustment rules in
subparagraph (B).
